1. Registration Process

Step 1: Visit the Website Go to Nextratedartist.com and click “Sign Up as an Artist or login” with your existing username and password on the homepage.

Step 2: Fill in Your Details Enter your full name, email address, and password. Confirm you’re a musical artist.

Step 3: Pay Registration Fee Total Fee: ₦3,500 (includes ₦1,000 registration + ₦2,500 for your first track upload).
- Select a payment method: Bank Transfer, Debit Card, or Online Payment. - Upload proof of payment if required.

Step 4: Verify Your Account - Check your email for a verification link. Click to activate your account.
Note: After registration, proceed to set up your Artist Dashboard.

2. Setting Up Your Artist Dashboard

Your dashboard is your personalized hub to manage music, bio, and promotions.

A. Profile Setup 1. Click “Edit Profile” on your dashboard.
2. Fill in:

  • Biography: Share your musical journey, inspirations, and achievements.
  • Education Background: Music training, degrees, or workshops.
  • Early Life: How you discovered your passion for music.
  • Studio/Genre: Add your music genre and studio details.

    B. Uploading Music

  • 1. Click “Upload Track” on your dashboard.

  • 2. Pay ₦2,500 per track (via the same payment methods).

  • 3. Upload your track file (MP3/WAV) and add details:

    • - Track title
    - Genre
    - Release date
    - Cover art


    Key Rules:


  • - Only 1 track can be uploaded at a time.

  • - Tracks stay on the homepage for 1 month, then move to your dashboard.

  • - Your dashboard holds up to 10 tracks. Delete old tracks to add new ones.




    3. Blog Page: Sharing Your Stories


  • 1. Go to the “Blog” section on your dashboard.

  • 2. Click “Create Post” to:
  • - Write news (e.g., upcoming albums, collaborations).

  • - Upload photos (studio sessions, events).

  • - Share behind-the-scenes stories.



Note: Blog posts must adhere to community guidelines.
